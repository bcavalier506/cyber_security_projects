🏗️ Sprint 01: Secure Network Architecture & Design
Objective: To redesign a fictitious organization's flat network into a modernized, segmented architecture to improve security posture, redundancy, and traffic management.

🛠️ Technical Process
Network Segmentation: Reorganized the corporate network into logical zones (Manufacturing, Parts Storage, Office Staff, and IT Staff) to limit lateral movement in the event of a breach.

Redundancy & Failover: Implemented a dual ISP configuration (ISP 1 & ISP 2) to ensure 100% uptime for critical business operations.

Security Layering: Integrated perimeter and internal firewalls to protect sensitive departmental assets and the core server infrastructure.

Wireless Security: Designed a segmented Wi-Fi infrastructure with dedicated access points for different staff levels, ensuring guest or unauthorized traffic is isolated from the production environment.

📄 Project Deliverables
Network_Architecture_Diagram.png: The visual representation of the redesigned corporate infrastructure.

Network_Engineering_Report.pdf: A comprehensive document outlining the logic behind the segmentation, hardware choices, and security implementations.

🧰 Skills Demonstrated
Secure Architectural Design: Understanding how to separate "Crown Jewel" assets (like Manufacturing) from general office traffic.

Resource Mapping: Organizing IP schemes and hardware placement for a scalable business environment.

Business Continuity: Designing for high availability through ISP and router redundancy.
