📚 Sprint 07: Professional Development & Certification Mastery
Objective: To design and execute a high-intensity study framework for the CompTIA Security+ certification, synchronized with an 8-month cybersecurity bootcamp curriculum.

🛠️ Study Methodology & Habits
Accelerated Learning: Managed a rigorous study schedule alongside a full-time, 8-month intensive bootcamp, balancing hands-on labs with theoretical exam objectives.

Active Recall & Spaced Repetition: Developed a comprehensive digital flashcard system covering all five Security+ domains: General Security Concepts, Threats/Vulnerabilities, Architecture, Operations, and Governance.

Technical Synthesis: Created "Field Guides" and comparative spreadsheets to master complex topics like Asymmetric Cryptography, IAM Protocols (SAML/OAuth), and Cloud Security Models.

Simulated Exam Environment: Utilized high-fidelity practice tests to master time management and technical question analysis under pressure.

🏆 The Results
The dedication to these study habits resulted in a successful first-attempt pass of the CompTIA Security+ (SYO-701) exam by the conclusion of the bootcamp. This certification serves as a baseline validation of my ability to implement industry-standard security solutions and follow best practices in risk management.
