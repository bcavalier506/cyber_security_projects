🏗️ Sprint 06: Infrastructure Modernization & Network Hardening
Objective: To develop a strategic "Modernization Roadmap" for Yagé Botanicals, a multi-state enterprise with significant technical debt. The goal was to harden the network and transition to scalable virtualization/cloud solutions to increase the company’s valuation for private equity acquisition.

🛠️ Strategic Modernization Phases
Asset Discovery & Inventory (CMDB): Designed a process to replace manual record-keeping with a formal Configuration Management Database (CMDB). Implemented automated network discovery to identify all "shadow" servers and hardware assets.

Network Hardening & Hygiene: * Addressed critical failures including expired UTM firewall subscriptions.

Proposed a transition from slow sDSL connections to high-speed fiber to eliminate productivity bottlenecks.

Standardized Wi-Fi security to replace "honor system" access with enterprise-grade authentication.

Virtualization & Cloud Migration: * Evaluated "On-Prem vs. Cloud" journeys to reduce technical debt.

Recommended virtualizing the "chaotic" server closet to reduce physical footprint, lower energy costs, and improve disaster recovery.

Operational Stability: Developed a plan for a Managed SOC (Security Operations Center) and NOC (Network Operations Center) integration to ensure 24/7 uptime and threat detection without overextending the modest internal IT budget.

Policy Governance: Drafted essential IT policies (AUP, Incident Response, and Hardware Lifecycle) to provide the "administrative maturity" required by prospective buyers.
